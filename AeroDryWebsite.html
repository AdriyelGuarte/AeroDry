Description of AeroDry

Drying clothes has always been a tedious chore for everyone. From the very start, people used the natural flow of air to dry their clothes. Unfortunately, it is not so efficient since rain often stops the drying process. Since then, dryer machines have been invented, but they are costly and use a significant amount of electricity. That is why our team decided to create a device that can use the natural flow of air to our advantage and be safe from the risk of clothes getting wet from rain. Not only that, it also dries the clothes and collects them automatically. This innovative device is called “AeroDry”.

Purpose of AeroDry

This project's purpose is to make people's lives easier when it comes to housework. People don't need to run in panic when it suddenly starts to rain because AeroDry can automatically retract the clothes to a dry place and ensure they won’t get wet. It aims to lighten the burden of laundry, with its functionality still doing its duty and not hindering the actual housework of a person. The product can be connected via smartphones, making the whole automatic retracting process fun and stress-relieving.

Functions of AeroDry

AeroDry is an innovative drying system designed to efficiently dry clothes using the natural flow of air with no risk of getting wet from unexpected weather changes. The project is built around an Arduino UNO microcontroller, which serves as the brain of the system. It is programmed using C++ through the Arduino IDE to control various functions of the device.

AeroDry operates through a three-step system, known as R.A.S., which stands for RainGuard, AeroBoost, and SafeCollect:

  • RainGuard: AeroDry detects rain and retracts the clothes into a safe, dry space with an LED indicator.
  • AeroBoost: The system dries the clothes automatically with the use of a fan for a set amount of time.
  • SafeCollect: AeroDry features an automatic cloth collector, using a claw to collect the clothes when they're dried.
